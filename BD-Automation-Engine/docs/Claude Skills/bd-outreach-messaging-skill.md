# BD Outreach Messaging - The PTS Formula

Transform cold calls into warm introductions using the 6-step BD Formula with program-specific personalization, pain points, and past performance alignment.

**Keywords**: BD formula, outreach, messaging, cold call, warm introduction, personalization, pain points, past performance, email template, call script

## The 6-Step BD Formula

Every outreach follows this exact sequence to differentiate from staffing competitors:

### Step 1: Personalized Message
Role-specific icebreaker demonstrating knowledge of their work/program.

**DO:**
- Reference their specific site, team, or recent activity
- Show understanding of their operational context
- Mention specific program or mission they support

**DON'T:**
- Use generic "I saw your profile" openings
- Start with company pitch
- Lead with what you're selling

## Message Templates

### LinkedIn Connection Request (300 char limit)
```
Hi [NAME] - I noticed your work on [PROGRAM] at [LOCATION]. 
PTS supports similar GDIT programs with cleared [ROLE] talent. 
Would love to connect and learn about your team's mission.
```

### LinkedIn InMail
```
Subject: [PROGRAM] Staffing Support - [LOCATION]

Hi [NAME],

I noticed you're supporting [PROGRAM] at [LOCATION]. Given the 
[PAIN POINT - e.g., "high ISR tempo" / "modernization push"], 
I wanted to reach out.

PTS has been a GDIT staffing partner since 2018, supporting 
[RELEVANT PROGRAM] with [ROLE TYPE] talent. We're currently 
helping similar teams fill [JOB TITLE] positions.

Would a 15-minute call next week work to discuss how we might 
support your team?

Best,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Cold Call Script
```
"Hi [NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] from Prime Technical Services. 
I'm calling about your [PROGRAM] work at [LOCATION].

[PAUSE FOR RESPONSE]

I understand your team is dealing with [PAIN POINT], and I noticed 
you have open positions for [JOB TITLES].

needs.

send some information first?"
```

## Tone Guidelines by Tier

| Tier | Tone | Length | Focus |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| 5-6 (ICs) | Friendly, curious | Short | Mission, team dynamics |
| 4 (Managers) | Collaborative | Medium | Solutions, capabilities |
| 3 (PMs) | Professional, data-backed | Medium | Results, past performance |
| 1-2 (Execs) | Strategic, concise | Short | Value, partnership |
