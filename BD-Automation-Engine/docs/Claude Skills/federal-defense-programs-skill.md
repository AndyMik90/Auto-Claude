## BD Opportunity Signals

Look for these indicators of BD opportunity:

1. **Hiring Activity**
   - Multiple open positions at same site
   - Leadership positions posted (PM, Site Lead)
   - New capability areas mentioned

2. **Contract Events**
   - Option year approaching
   - Recompete announced
   - Task order modifications

3. **Program News**
   - Budget increases
   - Mission expansion
   - New technology adoption

4. **Pain Point Indicators**
   - Long-unfilled positions
   - Multiple postings for same role
   - Urgency language in job descriptions
