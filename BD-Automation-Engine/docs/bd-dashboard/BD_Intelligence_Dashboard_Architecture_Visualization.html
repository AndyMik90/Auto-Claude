üìù The 6-Step BD Formula

Every outreach follows this exact sequence to differentiate PTS from staffing competitors.

1
Personalized Message

Role-specific icebreaker demonstrating knowledge of their work/program. Reference their specific site, team, or recent activity.

2
Current Pain Points

Program-specific challenges sourced from HUMINT. "I understand [PROGRAM] teams are dealing with [PAIN POINT]..."

3
Labor Gaps & Open Jobs

Current vacancies at their location showing awareness of their hiring challenges. Cross-reference job scrapes.

4
PTS Past Performance with GDIT

Direct partnership history: BICES, GSM-O II, NATO BICES. Establishes credibility immediately.

5
Relevant Past Performance to Program

Similar mission experience: SOCOM JICCENT (ISR), Platform One (USAF DevSecOps), DISA JRSS (Network).

6
Past Performance Relevant to Job Title

Match PTS capabilities to their team function. If they need Network Engineers ‚Üí PTS DISA/coalition experience.
